Our Story

Passion Plated,
Every Night

Ember & Oak was born from a singular obsession: to honour the rawness of fire and the patience of slow cooking. Chef Marco Reyes trained under three Michelin-starred kitchens across Europe before returning home to build something unforgettable.

Every dish is a meditation. Every ingredient is sourced within 100 miles. Every evening is a ritual.
